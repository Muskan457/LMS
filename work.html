My User Interaction

  • Search for books and resources: Patrons can search the library's entire catalog by title, author, subject, or keywords.
  • Check real-time availability: They can see whether a specific book is on the shelf, on loan, or available for reservation.
  • Manage their account: A logged-in user can view their borrowing history, track due dates, and see any outstanding fines.
  • Place holds and reservations: If a book is currently checked out, a patron can place a hold on it and be notified when it becomes available.
  • Renew borrowed items: Most modern systems allow users to renew a book online before its due date, provided no other user has reserved it.
  • Receive notifications: The system can send automated email or SMS notifications for due date reminders, overdue fines, and reservation statuses.
